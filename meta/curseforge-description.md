<h2 style="text-align:center">Aquamirae expands your world with a frozen ocean biome – the Ice Maze above and a haunting Ship Graveyard beneath. Discover its unique ecosystem, progression, ambience, and lore as you uncover the fate of Captain Cornelia and survive the horrors lurking below the ice.</h2>

<br>

<p style="text-align:center"><img width=90% src=https://i.imgur.com/2Rn2RuE.png></p>

_The once endless ocean, the waters of which were raised from the depths by gusty winds, the ocean that was sailed by brave voyagers and fearsome flotillas of pillagers, is now chained in ice and snow._

_Frozen in time… The ships of those who were unlucky froze in the merciless ice, at the same moment terrible creatures crawled out of the abyss into the light of day. Through the blizzard, only the rare sounds of the horns of those who still hope for rescue are heard. What could transform the once unbridled ocean so drastically?_

<br>

- ❄️ <span style="color:#CED4D9;">**Frozen Biome: Ice Maze**</span> – Explore a deadly ice labyrinth above and a mysterious ship graveyard below.
- 🌊 <span style="color:#CED4D9;">**World Friendly**</span> – Spawns naturally in the Deep Frozen Ocean, fully compatible with world generation mods.
- 🏚️ <span style="color:#CED4D9;">**Structures to Explore**</span> – From small camps to huge frozen wrecks, packed with secrets and loot.
- 🎧 <span style="color:#CED4D9;">**Immersive Atmosphere**</span> – Custom sounds, music, and drifting snow bring the Ice Maze to life.
- 🦑 <span style="color:#CED4D9;">**Monsters of the Deep**</span> – Face terrifying creatures hiding both in the maze and under the ice.
- 👻 <span style="color:#CED4D9;">**Captain Cornelia**</span> – Battle the ghostly captain with smooth animations, deadly attacks, and her own tragic story.
- ⚔️ <span style="color:#CED4D9;">**Unique Weapons**</span> – Find and use powerful items with special abilities.
- 🛡️ <span style="color:#CED4D9;">**Armor Sets**</span> – Mix and match pieces to unlock special set effects.
- 🍲 <span style="color:#CED4D9;">**Food & Trinkets**</span> – Enjoy new consumables, curiosities, and decorative treasures.
- ⚙️ <span style="color:#CED4D9;">**Config Support**</span> – Easily tweak generation, difficulty, and features to fit your world.
